Free Offline Video Converter & Media Studio

Utility Studio's Media Engine is a powerful, browser-based tool designed to format, compress, and edit video and audio files. Unlike traditional converters, our engine utilizes WebAssembly and FFmpeg to process your media entirely offline.

🔒 100% Private Processing

Your privacy is guaranteed. Because the engine runs locally inside your browser, your sensitive videos and audio tracks are never uploaded to a remote server.

⚡ Core Features

  • Convert MP4, MKV, AVI, WebM, and GIF
  • Extract high-quality MP3 or AAC audio
  • Visually trim and crop video timelines
  • Mix custom audio tracks over video files
