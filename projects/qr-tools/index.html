Why use an offline QR Code generator?

Typing your home Wi-Fi password or personal contact details into a free online generator sends your private data to a remote server. Utility Studio generates static, untracked QR codes entirely locally, ensuring your information stays secure. Read more about the privacy risks of cloud-based QR tools.
