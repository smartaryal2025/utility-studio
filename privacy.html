Privacy Policy

TL;DR: Your Data Stays on Your Device

Utility Studio operates entirely within your web browser. We do not have servers that process your files. Your PDFs, Images, Videos, and text never leave your computer or mobile phone.

1. Local Processing

All heavy processing tasks (such as video conversion via FFmpeg, image cropping, and PDF merging) are executed locally using client-side JavaScript and WebAssembly. No files are uploaded to any external server or cloud storage provider by Utility Studio.

2. Offline Capabilities

Our application utilizes Service Workers to cache application assets (HTML, CSS, JS) locally. This allows the application to function without an internet connection, further ensuring that your workflow remains entirely private and contained on your hardware.

3. Analytics and Tracking

We do not track individual user behavior, file contents, or personal usage data. Any analytics implemented (if applicable) are strictly limited to anonymized, aggregated page views to ensure website health and performance.

4. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at support@kishoraryal.com.np
